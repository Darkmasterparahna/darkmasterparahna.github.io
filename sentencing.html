The YCJA

Example of the YCJA justice process vs. the Criminal Code of Canada process:
(Green is doesn't charge, Red is does charge)

To simplify the chart above: To begin there has to be a young person who broke the law. After they are accused of breaking the law, the police officer can either choose to charge or not to charge him.

If they choose to not charge him, they will give him a warning and send a letter to their parents

Otherwise, if the crime is serious or there are other circumstances they will go to court.

They will then go to a Judge where they will get a sentence. Such as Community service or counselling
